Intensive Ukulele Courses

This complete ukulele course is aimed at both right-handed and left-handed people, we are going to explain from how to hold your ukulele to how to make your own strums, it is a course that is focused so that at the end of it you can have the bases to start playing any song you want. It does not matter if you have never played any other instrument, this course is designed for beginners who are looking for a more organized method to learn to play.
